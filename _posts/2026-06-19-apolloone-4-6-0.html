--- layout: post title: "ApolloOne 4.6.0" date: 2026-06-19 23:45:00 +0000 summary: "Support for new cameras. Automatic Lens Correction. Enhancements and bug fixes." ---
  • NEW! Official support for Sony A7R VI and Panasonic DC-L10 via RawBridge™
  • NEW! Automatic Lens Correction — automatically corrects geometric distortion, vignetting, and lateral chromatic aberration using each camera's own embedded lens profile, keeping the decoder's native color and tone. Coverage varies by brand (see RAW Processing): distortion for Canon RF, Sony, Nikon Z, Fujifilm, Olympus / OM System, and Panasonic; vignetting for all of these except Panasonic; and chromatic aberration for Sony, Nikon Z, Fujifilm, Olympus / OM System, and Panasonic.
  • NEW! Support for newer cameras on older versions of macOS via RawBridge™ — full Apple-RAW pipeline coverage on older systems for Canon EOS R6 Mark III, EOS R8, EOS R10, EOS R50, EOS R50 V, and EOS R100; Sony A7 V and ZV-E10 II; Nikon Z6 II, Z7 II, and ZR; Fujifilm X-E5 and X-T30 III; Olympus E-M1 Mark II; and Panasonic DC-G100, DC-L10, DC-S5, DC-ZS80, and DC-ZS95. LibRaw fallback for the oldest systems.
  • Copy To / Move To enhancements and bug fixes.
  • Updated ExifTool to version 13.59.
  • Various enhancements and bug fixes.
