--- layout: post title: "Camera RawX 2.5.0" date: 2026-05-12 18:05:00 +0000 summary: "Enhanced HDR RAW rendering. Improved camera support." ---
  • Hasselblad X2D II 100C support — Quick Look previews and thumbnails now render 3FR files from the X2D II 100C body via Apple's native RAW decoder (macOS Sonoma or later required).
  • Unified Quick Look rendering on every display — a custom filmic tone curve and hue-masked sky/sunset chroma enhancement now run on both EDR-capable and standard displays, so Quick Look previews match ApolloOne's on-canvas look regardless of HDR mode. Visibly richer skies, sunsets, and clouds.
  • Enhanced HDR Quick Look rendering — adaptive tone mapping measures each image's actual highlight peak and calibrates to your display's current HDR headroom, for faithful highlight detail on every EDR-capable Mac.
  • Updated Shadow & Highlight filter to match ApolloOne 4.5.0 (smoother lift across mid-shadows on dense foliage; highlight knee preserves blue-sky brightness during recovery).
  • Refined HDR Quick Look tone mapping — caps the source headroom at the panel's potential headroom, preventing midtone dimming on EDR-constrained displays (Studio Display) when previewing high-peak files.
  • Quick Look filmic chain ships in a neutral configuration. Users wanting Highlight Contrast, Sky Enhancer, or Sunset Enhancer tuning can edit RAW files in ApolloOne, where these adjustments live as Quick Edit sliders.
