--- layout: post title: "Camera RawX 2.6.0" date: 2026-06-19 23:42:00 +0000 summary: "Support for new cameras. Improved camera support for older macOS." ---
  • NEW! Official support for Sony A7R VI and Panasonic DC-L10 via RawBridge™.
  • NEW! Support for newer cameras on older versions of macOS via RawBridge™ — full Apple RAW pipeline coverage on older systems for Canon EOS R6 Mark III, EOS R8, EOS R10, EOS R50, EOS R50 V, and EOS R100; Sony A7 V and ZV-E10 II; Nikon Z6 II, Z7 II, and ZR; Fujifilm X-E5 and X-T30 III; Olympus E-M1 Mark II; and Panasonic DC-G100, DC-L10, DC-S5, DC-ZS80, and DC-ZS95. LibRaw fallback for the oldest systems.
  • Various enhancements and bug fixes.
